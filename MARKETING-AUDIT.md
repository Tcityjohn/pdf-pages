## Problems

### High: Misleading Keywords Attracting Wrong Traffic
`scan` and `ocr` â€” this app does NOT scan documents or perform OCR. Someone searching "PDF scanner" wants to photograph paper documents. Someone searching "OCR" wants text extraction from images. Your app splits and reorders pages. These keywords attract people who will download, discover it doesn't scan, and either leave a bad review or immediately delete.

This is the same class of problem as Voice Crumbs ranking for "crumbs" â€” impressions from irrelevant searches.

### Medium: Feature Words Wasting Space
`secure` and `offline` = 14 characters on features nobody searches for.
`view` = 4 characters on a word too generic to be useful (everything can "view").

### Medium: Missing Screenshots

### Low: Only 4 iPhone Screenshots
