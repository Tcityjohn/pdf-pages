Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2026

Overview

Voice PDF Extractor ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or documents.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. All PDF processing happens entirely on your device. Your documents never leave your device and are not uploaded to any server.

Permissions Used

The App requests the following permissions to provide its features:

  • Microphone: Used for voice commands to select pages (e.g., "pages 1 to 5"). Voice data is processed locally using Apple's Speech Recognition framework and is not stored or transmitted by the App.
  • Speech Recognition: Enables voice-to-text conversion for hands-free page selection. This uses Apple's on-device speech recognition.
  • Photo Library: Optional access to save extracted pages as images if you choose to export them.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Apple's built-in Speech Recognition framework. Apple may process voice data according to their own privacy policy. For more information, see Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

The App stores minimal data locally on your device:

  • Usage count for the free tier (number of extractions used)
  • Purchase status if you upgrade to unlimited extractions

This data is stored locally using iOS secure storage and is not transmitted anywhere.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
