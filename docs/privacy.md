# PDF Pages Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026-02-04

## Overview

PDF Pages is a mobile application that lets you select and extract pages from PDF documents. This privacy policy explains how our app handles data and respects your privacy.

## Data Collection

**Your PDF files never leave your device.** All PDF processing happens locally on your device. No files are uploaded to any server.

## Microphone & Speech Recognition

- The app offers optional voice commands for hands-free page selection
- Speech recognition is processed on-device using Apple's Speech framework
- **No audio is recorded or stored**
- You can use the app fully without enabling microphone access

## Third-Party Services

### Analytics (PostHog)
- We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app
- This includes: app opens, feature usage, device type, and iOS version
- This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally
- You can opt out of personalized tracking in your device settings

### In-App Purchases (RevenueCat)
- We use RevenueCat to process in-app purchase transactions
- Only transaction data necessary for purchase processing is collected
- No personal financial information is stored within the app
- Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- **Your PDF files** — all processing is local, never uploaded
- **Personal information** — no names, email addresses, or personal identifiers
- **Location data** — we do not access your location
- **Contacts or photos** — we do not access your contacts or other personal data

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Anonymous analytics data is shared only with PostHog for analytics processing and RevenueCat for subscription management.

## Data Retention

Analytics data is retained for up to 12 months. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.

## Children's Privacy

PDF Pages is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## User Controls

- You can deny microphone access and still use all core features
- You can upgrade to premium for unlimited extractions
- The app does not require or store any personal identification information

## Contact

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us through the App Store.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Please review it periodically.
