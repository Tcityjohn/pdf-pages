Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2026

PDF Pages ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

Analytics Data

We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app. This includes:

  • App opens and screen views
  • Feature usage (e.g., number of pages extracted)
  • Device type and iOS version
  • Crash reports

This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Purchase Information

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat processes:

  • Subscription status
  • Purchase history
  • Anonymous user identifiers

Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment details.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your PDF files – All PDF processing happens locally on your device. Your files are never uploaded to any server.
  • Personal information – We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personal identifiers.
  • Location data – We do not access your location.
  • Contacts or photos – We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Anonymous analytics data is shared only with:

  • PostHog – for analytics processing
  • RevenueCat – for subscription management

Data Retention

Analytics data is retained for up to 12 months. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

PDF Pages is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: support@pdfpages.app
